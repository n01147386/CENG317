      • According to my original plan, I was going to use plywood to construct a demo house, but then I spoke to a friend who suggested to go with foam board instead because it's way much easier material to work it. Now I have to admit that the cutting job was not perfect but it's good enough to serve its purpose - to demonstrate door motions and report events. Given that this course is not about arts and crafts nor woodwork but rather computer/electrical engineering oriented then I don't think this change of plan should be an issue.
